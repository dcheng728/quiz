### trig definitions for right triangle
difficulty: basic
labels: definitions

Define $\sin$, $\cos$, and $\tan$ for a right triangle with sides $A$ (opposite), $B$ (adjacent), and $C$ (hypotenuse).

---

$\sin a = A/C$, $\cos a = B/C$, $\tan a = A/B = \sin a / \cos a$

---

On the unit circle, $(\cos\theta, \sin\theta)$ gives the coordinates of a point at angle $\theta$. The Pythagorean theorem $A^2 + B^2 = C^2$ immediately gives $\sin^2 + \cos^2 = 1$.
===

### Pythagorean identities
difficulty: basic
labels: identities

State the Pythagorean identity and the two related identities involving $\sec$ and $\csc$.

---

$\sin^2 x + \cos^2 x = 1$, $1 + \tan^2 x = \sec^2 x$, $1 + \cot^2 x = \csc^2 x$

---

All three follow from dividing $\sin^2 + \cos^2 = 1$ by $1$, $\cos^2$, or $\sin^2$ respectively.
===

### angle addition formulas
difficulty: basic
labels: identities

State the angle addition formulas for $\sin(x \pm y)$ and $\cos(x \pm y)$.

---

$\sin(x \pm y) = \sin x \cos y \pm \cos x \sin y$ and $\cos(x \pm y) = \cos x \cos y \mp \sin x \sin y$

---

These are essential for decomposing wave superpositions and deriving beat frequencies. They follow directly from Euler's formula: $e^{i(x+y)} = e^{ix}e^{iy}$.
===

### double-angle formulas
difficulty: basic
labels: identities

State the double-angle formulas for $\sin 2x$ and $\cos 2x$.

---

$\sin 2x = 2\sin x \cos x$. $\cos 2x = \cos^2 x - \sin^2 x = 2\cos^2 x - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2 x$.

---

The $\cos 2x$ forms are especially useful: rearranging gives the half-angle substitutions $\cos^2 x = (1 + \cos 2x)/2$ and $\sin^2 x = (1 - \cos 2x)/2$, which appear in time-averaged intensities.
===

### trig values at standard angles
difficulty: basic
labels: special-values

What are $\sin$, $\cos$, and $\tan$ of the standard angles $0, \pi/6, \pi/4, \pi/3, \pi/2$?

---

$\sin: 0, \frac{1}{2}, \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}, \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, 1$. $\cos: 1, \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}, \frac{1}{2}, 0$. $\tan: 0, \frac{\sqrt{3}}{3}, 1, \sqrt{3}, \infty$.

---

These values should be known by heart. They follow from the geometry of 30-60-90 and 45-45-90 triangles.
===
